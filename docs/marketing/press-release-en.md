## RunVie Launches Vietnam's First AI-Coached Running App in Vietnamese

### Locally-built fitness app combines Vietnamese-language AI coaching, Vietnamese food calorie database, and tight integration with local races - aiming to get 10 million Vietnamese moving in five years.

**HANOI, VIETNAM - [Launch Date]** - RunVie today officially launched on the App Store and Google Play, becoming the first running and fitness app built natively for Vietnamese users with a fully Vietnamese-language AI coach. Unlike Western fitness apps available in Vietnam, RunVie speaks to users in three regional dialects (Northern, Central, Southern), tracks calories from over 2,000 traditional Vietnamese dishes, and integrates directly with 12 local marathon events.

During a 12-week closed beta with more than 5,000 testers, RunVie reported a 30-day retention rate of 28% - more than double the global fitness app average of approximately 12% (Sensor Tower, 2025).

### The problem: 70% of Vietnamese runners quit within the first 30 days

According to a RunVie-commissioned survey of 2,000 Vietnamese adults aged 22-45, 70% of those who began running quit within the first month. The top three reasons: (1) they did not know proper technique, (2) they lacked personalized guidance, and (3) existing apps did not understand local context - from diet to weather to running locations.

"We built RunVie for my mother," said **[Founder Name]**, Founder and CEO of RunVie. "She is 52, wanted to start walking to lose weight, but downloading any Western app left her confused. She told me, 'Why can't I have one that just speaks Vietnamese? I don't know what a cardio zone is.' That moment made us decide we would not build 'Strava for Vietnam' - we would build the product Vietnamese people actually deserve."

### The solution: Coach Vie - a personal trainer in every pocket

RunVie 1.0 ships with:

- **Coach Vie**, the AI running coach speaking Vietnamese in three regional accents, giving real-time guidance on form, breathing, and pacing.
- **Personalized training plans** for four goals: weight loss, first 5K, half marathon, full marathon. Plans adapt weekly based on user pace, schedule, and biometrics.
- **Vietnamese food calorie library** with 2,000+ dishes including pho, bun bo, banh mi, com tam - automatic intake estimation.
- **RunCoin & RunVie Mall**: Every kilometer earns RunCoins redeemable for vouchers from Coolmate, Pocari Sweat, Hoka, and 25+ partner brands.
- **Local community**: Virtual group runs, city-level leaderboards, and direct integration with Vietnamese running clubs.
