## Problem

Five million Vietnamese want to move more but every fitness app on their phone was built for someone else. Strava, Nike Run Club and Adidas Running ship English-only voice, do not recognize phở or bún bò calories, charge in dollars (USD 9.99 = 1.5% of a junior Saigon salary), drain 18% battery/hour, and glorify 5:00/km paces that shame the 70% of Vietnamese under 5,000 steps/day (WHO 2024). Obesity in Vietnam has risen 38% since 2010 (Ministry of Health). VnExpress Marathon registrations have tripled since 2019. The demand is escape velocity; the product gap is wide open.

## Solution

RunVie is a Flutter cross-platform app with three Vietnam-native pillars. First, **walking-first UX** — default 6,000-step goal, no global leaderboards, run mode unlocks gradually. Second, **AI Coach in natural Vietnamese** — Claude-powered chat that understands "hôm nay mệt," recommends phở gà recovery meals, adapts to Hanoi AQI 180 mornings, and gives voice cues in Vietnamese during runs. Third, **RunCoin** — coins earned per km, redeemable for Shopee, Grab, MoMo, Pocari, Coolmate vouchers; 70% of face value subsidized by brand partners, not RunVie burn. Apple Watch and Health Connect Android compatible.
