## Standard Seed Preferred Term Sheet

### Economic terms
- **Liquidation preference:** 1× non-participating preferred. Investor takes 1× back OR converts to common — whichever is greater. No participating / no multiple.
- **Dividends:** 6% non-cumulative, payable only when declared by board.
- **Anti-dilution:** Broad-based weighted average. (Reject full-ratchet; reject narrow-based.)
- **Pay-to-play:** None at seed (becomes standard at Series A).

### Control terms
- **Board composition:** 3 seats — Founder (1), Investor (1, lead seed), Independent (1, mutually agreed). Founder retains majority of independent appointment authority while owning >40%.
- **Protective provisions (Series Seed):** Standard — sell company, issue new senior preferred, change charter, increase ESOP, take debt >USD 250k.
- **Information rights:** Monthly dashboard (KPI), quarterly unaudited financials, annual audited financials (M24+).

### Transfer terms
- **Right of First Refusal (ROFR):** Company first, then preferred investors pro-rata.
- **Co-sale (tag-along):** Preferred can sell pro-rata alongside founder secondaries.
- **Drag-along:** Triggers on majority of preferred + majority of common approving change-of-control. Standard threshold.
- **Founder secondary:** Up to USD 150k secondary permitted at Series A (founder partial liquidity for personal runway).

### Vesting
- **Founder shares:** 4-year vest, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter. Already 50% vested at seed close (assuming 2-year founder track record at close).
- **ESOP grants:** 4-year vest, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter. 90-day post-termination exercise window (extend to 7 years for >2yr tenure — modern best practice).

## Notes on VN/SG Structure

- **Singapore Pte Ltd as holding co** — required for international investors (most VC funds cannot wire to a Vietnamese entity directly due to FX controls).
- **100% subsidiary in Vietnam** — operates the business, hires staff, holds IP licenses. Standard "Flip" structure pre-negotiated with seed counsel.
- **IP assignment:** Founder + all contractors + all employees sign IP assignment + confidentiality + non-solicit at hire. Vietnamese law enforceability of non-compete is weak — rely on IP assignment + trade secret protection instead.
- **Cap table software:** Carta or Pulley from seed close. Avoid spreadsheets after first investor wires money.
- **283 area form / Nghị định 122 reporting:** Required for SBV (State Bank of Vietnam) when foreign capital enters VN subsidiary. Counsel handles.
