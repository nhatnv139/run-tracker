## Data Room Access Protocol

1. Investor requests access via email.
2. Founder confirms genuine interest (15-min call before NDA).
3. NDA signed (mutual, 18-month term, standard template).
4. DocSend link issued with watermark + view tracking + expiry 30 days.
5. Sensitive folders (full financial model, contracts, customer list) require additional click-through acknowledgement.
6. Weekly review of who has viewed what — informs follow-up priority.

## Refresh Cadence

- Monthly: financials, cap table, KPIs, hiring plan
- Quarterly: roadmap, market analysis, competitive intel
- Event-triggered: new contracts, new hires, product launches, press, term sheet receipt
