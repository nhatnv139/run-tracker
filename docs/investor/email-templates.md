## General notes on investor email hygiene

- **Subject lines:** specific over clever. "RunVie seed — 15 min next week?" beats "Following up on our chat."
- **Length:** 150-300 words for outreach. 80-150 for follow-up. 200-400 for post-meeting.
- **Reply within 24 hours** to any investor email during active fundraise. Sets the tone of operational discipline.
- **Attachments:** always include a public 2-page exec summary; send deck only after first call confirms genuine interest.
- **CC discipline:** never CC multiple investors on the same email. Each gets a 1:1 thread.
- **Calendar:** maintain a single Notion or Airtable tracker — investor name, last contact date, status, next action. Update daily.
- **Tone calibration:** confident never arrogant. Specific never vague. Honest about risks always.
