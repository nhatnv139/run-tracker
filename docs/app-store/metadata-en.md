## 10. Localization notes

- No emoji in description (Apple guideline 4.0).
- Use "Tet" not "Tết" in English description (avoid diacritics confusion in non-VN locales).
- Brand names: Shopee, Grab, MoMo (preserve capitalization).
- Avoid unsupported superlatives ("best", "#1") — Apple flags under 2.3.7.
- USD pricing rounded to .99 convention.
